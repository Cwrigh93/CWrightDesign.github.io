Madden Ultimate Team Redesign

~HCI, UX, Wireframing, UI~

Introduction

Ultimate team is the cash crop of EA Sports thanks to players inability to resist micro-transactions in exchange for better teams. EA puts majority of it's time into the Madden Ultimate team mode but there are still areas in which it lacks and these areas are a nuciance to many players within the Madden Community. Every decision made was brought about thanks to input from fellow Madden community members within the Madden and MUT Reddit.

Target Demographic

Areas of emphasis

Though there are many areas in which the user experience could be improved I decided to focus on a few aspects including...

  • Team Adjustment Interface
  • Color Scheme of the interface
  • Choice of Roster/Depth chart to organize team
  • The inability to locate used power up passes
  • The complexity of finding and completing sets
  • Find archived sets by program name
  • Find archived sets by Desired Player
  • The menu layout for quicker primary selections

    Solutions

  • Implemented verticle scroll and a search function to quickly navigate to older sets
  • Added a ticker at the bottom of the interface to provide realtime NFL news
  • Restructured card operation to have Powerup passes be displayed in binder even while in use
  • Created a instance to display location and take user to card which uses wanted power ups
  • Provided users with immediate team adjusting option without having to make any selections
  • Provided users with multiple views for team adjusting in the form of a Roster/depth chart
