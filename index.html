MaxFit
HCI, UX

As a individual that is into fitness, I decided to practice using UX tools by making a fictionary app based on a fitness company. Hopefully I will be able to make use of these materials in the future when I can own my own fitness company.

3D Printed Prosthetic
HCI, UX, Participatory Design, User Testing

This is one of my favorite projects. It served as a capstone course and senior project during my final year as an undergrad. Not often do I get to use fabrication technology to make usable objects that will help better the lives of others.

This project was my introduction to User Experience Design as well as Human-Computer Interaction as a whole. Without this, I would have never thought about pursuing a career in UX Design.

Pictionary Digital
