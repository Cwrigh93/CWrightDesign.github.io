Maxfit

~HCI, UX, Wireframing~

Introduction

Fitness can be tricky. Some people are lazy while others are scared and confused. Schools don't give proper lessons on nutrition and can mislead students into thinking certain things are true while they aren't. The MaxFit app would help users take the step into the right direction when it comes to their health.

"If you have a friend who is interested in exercising you could go to the gym together. I know for a fact that having someone you trust with you makes it easier"

Solution

MaxFit incorporates the most crucial aspects of fitness. These include cardio, diet, and physical movement whether it be weight training, resistance, calisthenics, or plyometrics. The thing that separates this app from the rest is the versatility. There are apps such as BodySpace and MyFitnessPal that only do one thing and it's usually dedicated to that one task. Someone looking to start a health journey would have to download and use four or five different applications which can become annoying and tiresome. MaxFit is a one-stop for all one's fitness needs.
